# Federal Demand Primitive Engine North Star

Status: long-term ambition document

## Product Ambition

Build a reusable federal demand-intelligence engine that turns messy solicitation language into product, venture, and GTM primitives.

## Why This Could Matter

- federal demand is large but hard to compress into reusable product decisions
- requirement-kernel extraction can become a durable internal and external strategy engine

## Long-Term Product Shape

- Future workflow layers:
  - cross-agency compare
  - pre-RFP signal overlays
  - whitespace and demand-shift views

- Future outputs:
  - primitive library
  - opportunity clusters
  - build-here theses

## Expansion Logic

- Natural expansion: grow from decompiler to broader demand primitive engine
- Dangerous expansion: generic federal dashboard
- What must remain true: evidence-grounded reusable primitives

## Strategic Positioning

- Eventual category: federal demand intelligence
- Should not become: generic procurement workflow software
- Defensible if: the primitive taxonomy becomes meaningfully reusable
